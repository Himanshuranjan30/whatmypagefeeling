PagePulse - Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 8, 2025

Quick Summary: PagePulse analyzes webpage text for emotions. We don't collect personal data, store your browsing history, or track your activity.

1. Information We Process

Webpage Text Content: When you click "Analyze Content Emotion," we temporarily process the text content of the current webpage to identify emotional sentiment. This text is:

  • Sent to our secure AI analysis service for processing
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not linked to your identity or browsing history
  • Automatically deleted after analysis

User Preferences: We store your settings locally in your browser:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • This data never leaves your device

2. How We Use Information

  • Sentiment Analysis: Webpage text is analyzed by our AI service to identify emotional content
  • User Experience: Settings are saved to remember your preferences
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing habits or personal information

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

AI Processing Service: Webpage text is sent to our secure Netlify-hosted API for emotion analysis. This service:

  • Uses Google's Gemini AI for text analysis
  • Operates under strict data protection protocols
  • Does not retain your data after processing
  • Is accessed via encrypted HTTPS connections

No Data Sales: We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Data Security

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No personal identification information is collected
  • No browsing history is stored or tracked
  • Local settings are stored securely in your browser

5. Your Rights

  • Control: You control when analysis occurs by clicking the analyze button
  • Local Storage: You can clear extension data through browser settings
  • Transparency: Our extension code is open source and auditable

6. Children's Privacy

PagePulse does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension analyzes only webpage content that users actively choose to analyze.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Updates will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
